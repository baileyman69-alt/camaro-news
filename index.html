Local Constable Caught Burning Rubber for Christmas

By Staff Writer | December 24

Authorities confirmed late Tuesday night that a local constable was allegedly clocked traveling at 162 mph in a posted 55 mph zone while piloting a supercharged 1967 Chevrolet Camaro.

Witnesses reported hearing “what sounded like freedom” moments before the vehicle disappeared into the night, leaving nothing but tire smoke and shattered holiday peace.

Officials declined to comment on how a vehicle described as “aggressively American” managed to remain airborne over several county lines.

The investigation remains ongoing, though insiders suggest the only real crime may have been not inviting everyone else.
